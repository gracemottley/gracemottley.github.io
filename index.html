ABOUT GRACE

Grace is a senior journalism major at the Phillip Merrill School of Journalism. She has spent time writing and working on electoral politics. She is passionate about connecting people to new ideas, and telling stories in beautiful and innovative ways.

Grace has worked in a variety of areas. During her sophomore year, she covered facilities management and the University Senate for the Diamondback, the University of Maryland's independent student newspaper. During her time on staff, she assisted in covering the university's response to the death of student athlete Jordan McNair.

Grace also worked on Capitol Hill, serving as the intern assistant to the Scheduler of the late Congressman Elijah Cummings. After, she went on to work as a field intern in Iowa for Warren for President. She also served as a out-of-state precinct captain during the Iowa caucus.

Currently, Grace interns for Mothership Strategies, working to develop the digital fundraising prescence for clients like the late Rep. John Lewis and End Citizens United.
